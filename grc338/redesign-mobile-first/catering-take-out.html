Catering and Take Out

Our Services

At Alejandra’s Restaurant, we understand that sometimes you would rather just eat at home, but that doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice authentic cuisine. Whether you are planning a large event at a separate venue, or you simply want to eat at home, we offer Mexican food catering and takeout to satisfy the tastes of all our customers, their families, and their guests.

How It Works

When it comes to Mexican food takeout and catering, we make the process simple. To get started with your order.

  • Look over our online menu to make a decision about which authentic, fresh dish you would like.
  • Contact us to discuss your order including pick-up time, amount of dishes, etc.
  • Please call one week in advance for price quotes.
  • Pick up your catering or takeout order at our convenient location in Visalia
  • Give us a call at (559)636-3215 so we can take and confrim your order.
